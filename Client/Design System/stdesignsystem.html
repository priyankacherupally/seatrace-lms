Brand Voice

Clear
Plain language, no jargon, front-load key information.
"Save changes" not "Click here to save your changes"
Intelligent
Precise, data-backed, no fluff. We know our domain.
"98.4% uptime this month" not "Great performance!"
Approachable
Conversational, human. Use "you" and "we" naturally.
"Let's set up your dashboard" not "Initialise workspace"
Reliable
Same tone, spelling, and format everywhere — always.
Consistent CTA format: Verb + noun, e.g. "Download report"

Copywriting Guidelines

Do / Don't
✓ Do
"Save changes"
✕ Don't
"Click here to save your changes"
✓ Do
"3 items selected"
✕ Don't
"You have selected 3 items"
✓ Do
"Something went wrong. Try again."
✕ Don't
"Error 500"
✓ Do
"Free 14-day trial"
✕ Don't
"Try it for free for 14 days"
Rules
Headlines: Sentence case · Max 8 words · Lead with verb or number
Body: Max 2–3 sentences per paragraph · Active voice always
CTAs: Verb + noun — "Download report", "View dashboard", "Start trial"
Errors: Say what happened + what to do next. Never just "Error".

Usage Guidelines

Use Primary for the single most important action per screen (Save, Submit).
Use Secondary for supporting actions (Cancel, Export).
Use Tertiary for low-emphasis or repeated actions (links, inline edits).
Never place two Primary buttons side-by-side at the same level.
Maintain 8px spacing base across all layouts.
Always use semantic colors for status — never raw hex.
Logo must appear on every external-facing collateral and social post.
Disabled elements retain shape and size — only opacity or color changes.
